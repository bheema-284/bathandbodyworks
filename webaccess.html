ACCESSIBILITY COMPLIANCE

At Bath & Body Works, Inc we know that diversity, equity and inclusion makes us stronger – it’s one of our core values. Our commitment to creating an inclusive culture permeates everything we do, including the web.

With this in mind, we have taken steps to evolve and improve BBWInc.com, and we strive to comply with the best practices and standards defined by Section 508 of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) of the World Wide Web Consortium.

Our website is monitored and tested periodically by third-party accessibility consultants who are committed to helping us identify usability issues and implement solutions to further improve the accessibility of our site.
